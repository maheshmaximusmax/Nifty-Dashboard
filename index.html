Type
Δ
Delta
Rate of change of option price per ₹1 move in underlying. CE: 0 to 1 · PE: -1 to 0
Γ
Gamma
Rate of change of Delta. Highest at ATM. Accelerates P&L near expiry.
Θ
Theta
Time decay per day. Always negative for option buyers. Max decay last week before expiry.
Vega
Vega (ν)
Change in option price per 1% change in IV. Long options benefit from rising IV.
Rho
Rho (ρ)
Sensitivity to interest rate changes. Less significant for short-dated options.
IV
Impl. Vol
Market's expectation of future volatility. IV Crush after events. Trade IV not price.
